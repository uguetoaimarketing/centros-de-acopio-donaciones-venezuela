What to donate

We accept donations that are new, clean, sealed, in-date, and sorted by category. Medicine, chemicals, batteries/power banks, and medical supplies are accepted only if requested and approved by the receiving organization.
✅ Accepted
  • Water & food: bottled water, non-perishable food, energy bars, powdered milk, infant formula, baby food (sealed, in-date, easy-open, avoid glass)
  • Hygiene: soap, shampoo, toothpaste & toothbrushes, toilet paper, sanitary pads, diapers, wet wipes, deodorant
  • First aid (no medicine): gauze, bandages, medical tape, gloves, saline solution, basic kits, digital thermometers (no mercury)
  • Clothing & warmth: clean clothes in good condition, new socks, new underwear only, closed-toe shoes, blankets, sleeping bags
  • Shelter: tents, waterproof tarps, sleeping mats, thermal blankets
  • For children: diapers, blankets, books, small toys; bottles & pacifiers (new & sealed only)
⚠️ Accepted with conditions
  • Pet food: only if logistics allow or requested by an animal shelter — never ahead of food, water, medicine or shelter
  • Bleach, disinfectants, liquids, batteries & power banks: local drop-off only — not by courier or air (hazmat restrictions)
  • Medicine: only through authorized medical organizations or foundations with permits — never as a general pill collection
❌ Not accepted
  • Open or expired medicine; no box, lot or expiry date; loose samples
  • Antibiotics without medical coordination
  • Homemade food or expired food
  • Used underwear; broken shoes
  • Glass containers, aerosols, perfumes, strong chemicals
  • Unsorted or mixed boxes
